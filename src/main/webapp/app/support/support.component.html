Purefy Support

Welcome to the Purefy Support Center. Understanding the challenges that come with dopamine detox, we want to ensure you have access to the right resources, especially when it comes to your health and well-being.

Immediate Assistance

If you're experiencing severe distress or a medical emergency, please contact emergency services in your area immediately.

MENTAL HEALTH HELPLINE
If you're feeling overwhelmed, anxious, or need someone to talk to about your mental health, please reach out to the Mental Health Helpline https://www.nhs.uk/nhs-services/mental-health-services/ for further queries.

ADDICTION AND RECOVERY
For support with addiction-related challenges, including digital or substance use, visit https://www.nhs.uk/live-well/addiction-support/drug-addiction-getting-help/ to know more about support available.

EDUCATION AND WELLNESS
To enhance your understanding of mental and emotional health, delve into topics such as mindfulness, stress management, and balanced digital consumption. Seek out to books and articles to foster a holistic approach.

RESOURCES AND PUREFY TIPS
Further your exploration of mental health by engaging with diverse resources. Explore areas like emotional resilience, cognitive balance, and wellbeing strategies. Pursue a range of literature and studies that encourage wellness.
